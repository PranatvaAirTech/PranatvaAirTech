Who are we?

Pranatva Air Tech Private Limited, founded on the 27th of June, 2022, fervently undertakes the formidable challenge posed by vehicular emissions, a significant contributor to the escalating crisis of air pollution. Steeped in a steadfast commitment to the visionary goal of "Creating a Breathable Tomorrow," the company stands as a trailblazer, having pioneered an ingenious solution meticulously crafted to filtrate an array of pollutants. These encompass not only particulate matters but also vehicular emissions and carbon dioxide.

Pranatva Air Tech, with its focal expertise in Research & Development, Manufacturing, Installation, and Servicing, stands at the forefront of Ambient Air Purifiers. Driven by an unwavering dedication to providing avant-garde solutions, the company aspires to confront ambient air pollution with unparalleled efficacy. Its products, marked by meticulous design, serve as bulwarks against the encroachment of air pollutants in the surrounding environment, thereby nurturing a healthier and cleaner atmosphere for the benefit of all.

Vision

Creating a Breathable Tomorrow:
Pranatva Air Tech envisions a future where every breath is a testament to purity and well-being, relentlessly committed to 'Creating a Breathable Tomorrow' for generations to come.


Our Mission

Empowerment through Solutions:
Pranatva Air Tech is dedicated to providing innovative solutions aimed at reducing air pollution stemming from transportation, industrial activities, and household sources.


City Air Quality Guardians:

Restoring Balance:
Pranatva Air Tech is committed to bringing carbon dioxide (CO2) levels back to their pre-industrialization benchmarks, contributing to the restoration of environmental equilibrium and a sustainable future.

Problem

Air pollution, specifically P.M 2.5 and P.M 10, causes 7 million premature deaths annually, according to the World Health Organization. Daily exposure leads to serious health issues like stroke, COPD, tracheal and bronchial problems, lung cancers, aggravated asthma, and lower respiratory infections. Notably, 28% of fatal cases are due to lung cancer, while 82% result in heart failure. Increased P.M particle levels correlate directly with a higher risk of heart attacks.

Solutions

At Pranatva Air Tech, we are dedicated to ceaselessly pursuing sustainable, cost-effective, and cutting-edge technological solutions aimed at reducing pollutants and enhancing the quality of air for a brighter tomorrow. Our product line is meticulously crafted to filter out harmful components such as P.M 2.5, P.M 10, CO2, and ambient dust particles. These products prioritize sustainability by operating with minimal energy consumption and requiring minimal maintenance, aligning with our commitment to eco-conscious practices and efficient functionality.
